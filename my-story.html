My Story

At first I saw on YouTube someone crocheting a hat, then I was interested in making it.

The next day I bought yarn, after that I started crocheting a hat, but failed, and failed again.
Finally I studied on YouTube for a few days. and I managed to make a pom pom, after that I bought milk cotton yarn.

I wanted to make my first project which was a sunflower, I made various colors. Then I thought about selling my crochets,
I made a lot of knitting like animals, and fruits.

One day, I saw there was a free crochet class, I took the crochet class, and I managed to make amigurumi after that I could crochet, and I had made a lot of knitting that I would sell, I bought yarn again, and I made new products, because I like animals I make animal amigurumi like whales, dinos, bees, and others :)
